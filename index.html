FEATURES

WHY POP!_OS?

Workflow

Pop!_OS is designed for fast navigation, easy workspace organization,
and fluid, convenient workflow. Your operating system should
encourage discovery, not obstruct it.

LEARN MORE>>

Compatibility

Develop software out of the box with a vast array of libraries and tools at
your disposal. Pop!_OS uses APT and Flatpak package management, meaning
it’s easy to install, remove, and update all software on the OS.

LEARN MORE

Keep Your Computer Secure

Built with security at its core. Full-disk encryption comes standard, keeping
your data safe from unauthorized access. You’re in control of system updates,
installing them on your schedule to minimize disruptions. Privacy features
help protect your personal information, while firmware management ensures
your hardware stays up to date with the latest security patches.

LEARN MORE

Customization

Get comfy with an extensive, easy-to-find list of preferences. Set dock
orientation, window tiling, Super navigation, keyboard layout and shortcuts,
privacy controls, and more. Your computer, your tool, your way. YOURS.

LEARN MORE

OTHER FEATURES

Hybrid Graphics

Set phasers to stunning performance. Toggle between battery-saving and high-powered graphics, or launch individual apps on GPU power.

Gaming

Steam, Lutris, and GameHub create an incredible gaming experience in selection and performance. Let your game library roam free on one OS.

Do Not Disturb

Get rid of any and all notifications for however long you like. Cast out ye heathens, for there is many a work to be done!
